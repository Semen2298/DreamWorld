Privacy Policy

Welcome to Dream World. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: We collect payment details to process your orders. This includes credit card information, billing address, and other related information.
  • Technical Information: We collect technical information when you visit our website. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit on our site.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our services, such as the products you view or purchase, and your preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing Orders: To process and fulfill your orders, communicate with you about your orders, and provide customer service.
  • Personalization: To personalize your experience on our website, recommend products that may be of interest to you, and tailor our communications to your preferences.
  • Improving Our Services: To analyze and improve our website, services, and products, understand user behavior, and develop new features.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional offers, newsletters, and other marketing communications. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time.
  • Security: To protect our website and users from fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access, and to ensure the security of our network and information systems.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption technologies to protect your sensitive information during transmission.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your personal information to authorized employees and service providers who need it to perform their job functions.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.
  • Secure Storage: We store your personal information on secure servers that are protected by firewalls and other advanced security technologies.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, shipping, and marketing.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose your information to protect the rights, property, or safety of Dream World, our users, or others.

Your Choices

You have certain choices regarding the information we collect and how it is used:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information through your account settings on our website.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.
  • Delete Account: You can request the deletion of your account by contacting us at dreamworld@gmail.com.
  • Do Not Track: You can configure your browser settings to send a Do Not Track signal. However, please note that our website does not respond to Do Not Track signals at this time.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
